06 / ABOUT

Copy rooted in customer truth

I came to copywriting through customer experience — five years of sitting at the intersection of what brands say and how customers actually feel when they hear it. That gap? I've made it my whole thing.

I understand churn, loyalty, and the exact moment a customer decides whether to trust a brand. Because I've spent years managing the fallout when that trust breaks — and studying the strategies that keep it intact.

I'm also a published writer with over 7,000 readers on Wattpad, which means I've learned something most copywriters miss: readers know when you're performing for them versus actually speaking to them. I aim for the second one, every time.

01
The reader decides what the copy means.
I write for the person receiving it, not the brief. Customer psychology shapes every structural choice — from subject line to sign-off.
02
Clarity converts. Cleverness assists.
A great hook means nothing if the offer is buried. I prioritize information architecture — what do they need to know, and in what order?
03
Every email is part of a longer story.
I don't write standalone emails. I write with the full lifecycle in mind — because the tone of email 3 affects whether they open email 8.
